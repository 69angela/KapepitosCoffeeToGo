Cancellation of Payment Policy

1. Cancellation Period:

You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. After this period, cancellations will not be accepted.

2. How to Cancel Your Order:

  • Contact our Customer Support team at support@kapepito.com with the subject line "Order Cancellation" along with your order details.

3. Refund Process:

Once your cancellation is approved, we will initiate the refund process. The refund will be processed to the original payment method, and you can expect it within 24 hours.

4. Conditions for Cancellation:

  • Orders that have already been shipped cannot be cancelled.
  • If your payment was made using GCash or PayMaya, the refund will be processed back to the same payment method.

5. Refund Fees:

  • If a cancellation request is made after the allowed 24-hour period, a 10% cancellation fee may be deducted from the total amount of the order.
  • There will be no cancellation fee if the request is made within the first 24 hours.

6. Payment Methods:

For payments made using GCash or PayMaya, please ensure that you follow the instructions below when requesting a refund:

  • GCash/PayMaya: Refunds will be credited back to your GCash or PayMaya account.

7. Customer Support:

If you need further assistance with your cancellation request or refund process, feel free to contact our Customer Support team:
